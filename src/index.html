If it weren't for the dog my door alarm would've been flawless. The problem was that our family dog took pleasure in ripping up the carefully placed aluminum foil that was integral to the alarms circuitry. Today, I am still designing and building. But this time with a bigger tool belt. During my undergrad at Gonzaga I studied computer engineering to understand the building blocks of technology. Then at The University of Washington I received my masters in human-computer interaction
