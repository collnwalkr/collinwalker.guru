COLLIN WALKER

ux engineer + slackliner

If it weren't for the dog my door alarm would've been perfect. At nine years old, I had put together together a door alarm with repurposed wires, a speaker, aluminum foil, and a roll of duct tape. The problem was that our family dog took pleasure in ripping up the carefully placed aluminum foil that was integral to the alarms circuitry. Today, I am still designing and building. This time with a bigger tool belt after receiving a degree in computer engineering and masters in human-computer interaction. With this holistic understanding of technology and human behavior, I design and prototype solutions with the big picture in mind. Even if that big picture includes a spirited family pet.
