If it weren't for the dog my door alarm would have been perfect.

At nine years old, I had put together a door alarm with repurposed wires, a speaker, aluminum foil, and a roll of duct tape. The problem was that our family dog took pleasure in ripping up the carefully placed aluminum foil that was integral to the alarms circuitry.

Today, I am still designing and building. This time around I have a bigger tool belt after receiving a degree in computer engineering and masters in human-computer interaction + design. With this holistic understanding of technology and human behavior, I design and prototype solutions with the big picture in mind.

Even if that big picture includes a spirited family pet.
